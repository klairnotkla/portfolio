What is the significance of 2011?

In the whimsical world of dates, 20th November 2011 isn't just any day; it's the day when Clarissa, the 1996 vintage edition, burst forth into existence, and boy, did the calendar never look the same again!

What sets this Clarissa apart, you ask?

  1. Her family's woes are her constant companions.
  2. She's a kaleidoscope of personalities, each for a different audience.
  3. Her perspective? It's so wide, it's practically cosmic!
  4. She's the supreme ruler of all things Clarissa.
  5. She's not bonkers; just a splendid mix of silliness and goofiness!
